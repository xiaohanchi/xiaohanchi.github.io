Introduction

Welcome to my homepage! I am a third-year PhD student in the Department of Biostatistics at The University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center UTHealth Graduate School of Biomedical Sciences (GSBS), co-advised by Dr. Ruitao Lin and Dr. Ying Yuan. Before joining MD Anderson, I obtained both my MS and BS degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Dr. Zhangsheng Yu.

My current research interests lie in Bayesian clinical trial design, covariate adjustment and causal inference.
